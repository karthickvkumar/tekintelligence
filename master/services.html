About US

TekIntelligence is a Premier Mobile and Web Solutions company focused in creating rich user experience by combining deep design, development, and domain expertise. Our custom software solutions help clients connect people, processes, and data that enable them to make better business decisions and improve productivity that result in increased revenue.

Our Services

We have strong software delivery capabilities that ensure our clients achieve their business objectives faster and maximize their ROI. Our global team of skilled software architects, engineers and design experts, united by a passion for quality software applications, develop result oriented, performance-driven, user-friendly solutions that deliver outstanding value to our clients. We have flexible engagement models to suit varying needs of our clients with differing application capabilities, business needs and budget availability.

Our Cloud Competencies
